Welcome To Nass Enterprises Pvt ltd

At Nass Enterprises Pvt Ltd, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive waste management solutions designed to create a cleaner, more sustainable future. With expertise spanning waste collection, transportation, disposal, and recycling, we specialize in cleaning both industrial ETP tanks, refineries tanks, septic tanks, fumigation services, and the handling of scrap material. What We Do

We specialize in delivering comprehensive industrial and domestic waste management solutions. Our services are designed to ensure environmental sustainability, operational efficiency, and safety. We cater to a wide range of industries with tailored solutions.

Waste disposal

Nass Enterprises provides comprehensive waste disposal solutions for both industrial and domestic clients. Our environmentally responsible methods ensure safe collection, transportation, and disposal of hazardous and non-hazardous waste materials, complying with all regulatory standards to minimize environmental impact.

Tank Cleaning

We are specialized in ETP and all kind of refineries Tanks cleaning services catering to industrial and domestic tanks, ensuring thorough cleaning and maintenance. We use state-of-the-art equipment and safe cleaning practices to remove sludge, sediments, and hazardous materials, prolonging the life of your tanks and ensuring their efficient operation.

Fumigation

Our fumigation services are designed to protect your premises from pests and harmful microorganisms. We offer industrial and domestic fumigation solutions that comply with health and safety standards, using eco-friendly and effective treatments to ensure a pest-free environment.

Scrap Dealing

Nass Enterprises engages in the responsible collection, processing, and recycling of scrap materials. We buy and sell scrap metals, plastics, and other recyclables, promoting sustainable practices and reducing the waste burden on landfills.

Waste Water Treatment

Our wastewater treatment services are designed to help industries manage their water resources efficiently. We provide advanced solutions for treating wastewater, removing contaminants, and ensuring the water is safe for reuse or disposal, in compliance with environmental regulations.

Diesel Supply

We provide reliable diesel supply services to industries, ensuring an uninterrupted supply of high-quality fuel for machinery, vehicles, and backup generators. Our timely delivery service guarantees that your operations run smoothly, minimizing downtime.
